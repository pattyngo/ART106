LED Mask

LED mask created by circuts of LED diode lights connected on a perf board. The lights were sewn into a three layer face mask. A foam switch is used to control the lights. I chose to have the button wires going through the side of the mask so the button can be looped over the ear and hidden behind the wearer's head.
