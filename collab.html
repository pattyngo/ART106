Flora - Botany Sensory Mechanical Companion

Meet my companion, Flora. Flora is a robotic bee that follows me on my ventures into the wilderness within a fantasy world where there would be an abundance of diverse plants species. Flora’s main task is to protect me from toxic plants when I go foraging for ingredients. With a point of a finger, I can direct Flora to a nearby plant or fungus. Using her tarsal claws, Flora grabs a sample and ingests it to break the sample down. Her thorax contains compartments that can detect phytotoxins, as well as recognize the 70+ species of poisonous mushrooms. Her eyes light up green when a plant is safe to consume and red when she detects any toxins. Per my request, I can have Flora give me an audio analysis of each sample played through the speaker on the front of her head. Her antennae connect to Wi-Fi so she has access to a database of every plant species. Once each sample is processed, Flora converts the samples into fuel in order to power herself. Flora and I have a commensal relationship. Flora is self-sustaining due to her ability to produce her own energy and can fly away if she senses any physical danger. However, If I consume something without Flora’s analysis, I could potentially face harm.
