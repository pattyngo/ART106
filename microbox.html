Microbox

Speaker box made from a breadboard circut. My box contains the following components: 2 toggle switches, 2 potentiometers, 2 LED lights, and one speaker.The final box is Demon Slayer themed and plays the theme song from Demon Slayer. This anime has a group of four young heros that must work together to complete their missions, just like how all the components in my box work together to put on a performance.
